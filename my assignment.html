how to prepare my favourite meal

my favorite meal is yam and fried eggs

  1. yam
  2. eggs
  3. pepper
  4. salt
  5. sugar

    how to prepare yam

  • peel the yam
  • fill the cooking pot with some water
  • light the cooking gas and place the pot on it
  • add salt to it and sugar(optional)
  • allow it to boil for about 30min,then bring it down

    how to prepare eggs

  • break the eggs into an empty bowl
  • stir it well to mix eggs
  • blend your pepper to your taste
  • put your frying pan on the cooking gas
  • add some groundnut oil
  • set the cooking gas to minimum temperature
  • add salt,maggi to the mixed egg
  • pour the egg into the hot oil and allow to cook
